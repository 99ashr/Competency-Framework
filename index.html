Step 1 — Functional Head Questionnaire

Functional heads identify the Top 10 critical competencies for their department and set minimum Day-1 readiness levels.

Select your programme type (GET / MT) and department
Rate the importance of each competency (1–5 stars)
Mark your top 10 priority competencies with a checkbox
Set the minimum Day-1 proficiency level required
Submit — response is saved automatically for dashboard analysis
2

Step 2 — GET / MT Self-Assessment

Each trainee rates their current proficiency level against all competencies for their function. Responses are stored for gap analysis.

Trainee enters their name, ID, and department
Rates current proficiency using the 5-level scale (honest self-assessment)
Provides a brief evidence note for each rating
Manager validates ratings at 30 days using the same form
Submit — response stored for gap analysis
3

Step 3 — Gap Analysis

Automatically calculates the gap between desired level (set by functional head) and current level (self-assessment) per competency.

Gap = Desired Level − Current Level
Critical gap (≥3): Immediate mandatory training — before Day 30
Moderate gap (2): Priority training — Month 1–2
Minor gap (1): Development — self-paced LMS, Month 2–3
Exceeds (≤0): Strength — leverage as peer trainer / buddy
4

Step 4 — TNA & Training Modules

Gap scores drive the Training Needs Analysis, which maps to structured learning pathways across the 12-month programme.

Auto-generates TNA recommendations from gap severity scores
Maps to 6-phase learning pathway (Orientation → Hi-Po Evaluation)
Recommends specific training modules, vendors, and timelines
Aligns to 70-20-10 learning model and Kirkpatrick evaluation
Feeds into individual development plans (IDPs) for each trainee

Training Needs Analysis & Learning Pathways

The TNA translates gap analysis outputs into targeted learning interventions, aligned to the 6-phase GET & MT programme structure. All training follows the 70-20-10 learning model: 70% on-the-job experience, 20% mentoring and peer learning, 10% formal classroom or LMS-based training.
